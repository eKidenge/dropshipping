Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with us

Ordering

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping information and choose a payment method. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with the order details.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. After that, the order may have been processed and cannot be changed. Please contact our customer service immediately if you need to make changes.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping rates?

Shipping rates vary based on your location and order value. Standard shipping within Kenya starts from KSh 200. Free shipping is available for orders over KSh 5,000.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by location: Nairobi: 1-3 business days, Other major towns: 3-5 business days, Remote areas: 5-7 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries in East Africa including Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our Track Order page or directly on the courier's website to monitor your delivery.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Some items (like personal care products) cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons.

How do I return an item?

To initiate a return, log into your account, go to your orders, and select the item you wish to return. Follow the instructions to print a return label and drop off the package at any partner courier location.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your return. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button at the top of the page and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I update my personal information?

Log into your account and go to the Profile section. There you can update your personal details, change your password, and manage your addresses.
