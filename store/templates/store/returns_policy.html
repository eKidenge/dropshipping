Our Returns Policy

At QINEX KENYA, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we're here to help. This policy outlines our returns and refunds process.

Note: Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase. By placing an order with us, you agree to the terms of this returns policy.

30-Day Return Policy

You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In its original packaging
  • With all tags and labels attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Personal care items (cosmetics, skincare, etc.)
  • Intimate apparel (lingerie, swimwear, etc.)
  • Downloadable software or digital products
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items damaged due to misuse, accident, or neglect

Common Return Reasons

Wrong Size

Item doesn't fit as expected

Wrong Color

Color different from what was ordered

Damaged

Item arrived damaged or defective

Wrong Item

Received different product than ordered

Return Process

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Initiate Your Return: Log into your account, go to "My Orders," find the order containing the item you wish to return, and click "Return Item." Alternatively, contact our customer service team.
  2. Print Return Label: Once your return is approved, you'll receive a return shipping label via email. Print the label and attach it to your package.
  3. Pack Your Item: Securely pack the item in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  4. Ship Your Return: Drop off your package at any authorized courier location. Keep the receipt as proof of return shipping.
  5. Track Your Return: Use the tracking number provided to monitor your return's progress.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Refund Timelines

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days after processing
  • M-Pesa: 24-48 hours after processing
  • Bank Transfers: 3-5 business days after processing

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund within the specified timeframe:

  1. Check your bank account or payment method again
  2. Contact your credit card company or bank (processing times vary)
  3. If you've done both and still haven't received your refund, please contact us

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, the fastest way is to return the original item and place a new order for the desired item once your return is processed. This ensures you get the item you want as quickly as possible.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in cases where:

  • The item arrived damaged or defective
  • You received the wrong item
  • The return is due to our error

In these cases, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label and refund your full purchase price including original shipping costs.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at returns@quinexkenya.com with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged/defective item
  • A brief description of the issue

We will respond within 24 hours with instructions for return or replacement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return an item if I don't have the original packaging?

We strongly recommend using the original packaging to ensure the item is protected during return shipping. If you no longer have it, please ensure the item is securely packed in a sturdy box with appropriate padding.

How long does it take to process a return?

Returns are typically processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the item. You will receive an email notification once your return has been processed.

Can I return an item purchased with a gift card?

Yes, items purchased with gift cards can be returned. Refunds will be issued as a new gift card or store credit.

What if my return is lost in transit?

We recommend keeping the return shipping receipt with tracking information. If a return is lost in transit, please contact us with the tracking number, and we'll investigate with the courier.

Can I return multiple items in one package?

Yes, you can return multiple items in one package as long as they're from the same order. Please include all return forms in the package.
