Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our Track Order page or directly on the courier's website using the provided tracking number.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we currently ship to Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

What happens if I'm not home during delivery?

Our courier partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts fail, the package will be held at their nearest depot for pickup, or returned to us (additional shipping fees may apply for reshipment).

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Address changes are possible within 1 hour of placing your order. After that, the order may have been processed and cannot be modified. Please contact customer service immediately if you need to change your address.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available in select areas of Nairobi for orders placed before 12 PM. Additional charges apply. Check availability at checkout.
